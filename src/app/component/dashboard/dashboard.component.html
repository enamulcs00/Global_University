SERVICES OFFERING

Univ Global collaborates with overseas representatives and with Higher Education Institutions across number of countries to help them achieve their recruitment strategies. Our innovative technology portal simplifies the course search, course comparison and student application process. We help students discover and compare career opportunities across different countries and our online portal simplifies the process of comparison and applying, thereby helping them pursue their dream of studying abroad with ease.

Univ Global has collaborated with qualified and vetted representatives across the world with an aim to help their students to compare, choose and apply to a greater number of institutions of their choices in several countries.

HOW IT WORKS

We aim to make it as easy as possible for our users throughout the journey of course search and matching; applying to the programmes and universities that closely align with their interests. We help representatives and students accomplish this in just a matter of clicks with our automated technology.

When an application along with supporting documents are uploaded to our portal, our engines get to work analysing your documents before matching the documents against the entry criteria of partner universities in different countries.
